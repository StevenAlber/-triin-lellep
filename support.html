A Quieter Door

If the work has moved you, you can leave a trace.

Not every encounter belongs to the formal architecture of patronage or commission. Sometimes a voice simply finds you — at a strange hour, between languages, in a room that was not expecting it — and you wish to acknowledge that it reached you. This page is for that.

Five songs to listen to, below. Four ways to support the work, further down. Choose what feels honest. Nothing here is symbolic; every contribution directly funds the first chapter of the work — and the rooms still to come.

Listen First

Five songs, before you decide.

These are not from Opus I. They are earlier and lighter — songs Triin has written and recorded in studio across the last two years. They are the easiest way to hear what kind of voice this is, before reading another word.
