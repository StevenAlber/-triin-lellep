Some who hear the work do more than recognise it. They choose to carry it forward — to fund the room that will hold it, the film that will document it, the chapter that has not yet been performed.

If that is what you would like to do, this is the form for it. It is closer to patronage than to a purchase, though no document is signed. Only your name, and the work continuing.

Your name kept in the private archive of those who helped carry the work to its first chapter. Held by the studio with care, never made public.

Why this form

One hundred and fifty euros is more than a gesture. It is a contribution that helps cover one real cost of the first chapter — an hour of cinematography, a roll of film, the rental of a small instrument, a night for the photographer.

At this tier you are no longer simply listening. You are quietly underwriting a piece of the work's emergence. The studio holds this with seriousness. What you give does not pay Triin. It funds the chapter.

There is no expectation of further patronage beyond this single contribution. One door, opened once, fully.
